# Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

**Last Updated / 最后更新: 2026-01-30**

---

## English

### Introduction

MoodEcho ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our voice mood diary application.

### Data Collection and Storage

**Local Storage Only**: All your data is stored exclusively on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers.

The app stores the following data locally:
- Voice recordings (audio files)
- Transcribed text from your recordings
- Emotion analysis results
- App settings and preferences

### Voice and Speech Recognition

- Voice recordings are processed using Apple's on-device Speech Recognition framework
- Audio data never leaves your device during the standard analysis process
- Recordings are stored locally and can be deleted at any time

### Optional AI Analysis

If you choose to enable AI-enhanced emotion analysis:
- You must provide your own API key (OpenAI, Claude, DeepSeek, or Qwen)
- Text content (not audio) is sent to your chosen AI service for analysis
- We do not store or have access to your API keys beyond your device
- Please refer to the respective AI provider's privacy policy for their data handling practices

### Data Sharing

We do not:
- Sell your personal data
- Share your data with third parties
- Use your data for advertising
- Collect analytics or usage data

### Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time:
- Individual entries can be deleted from the app
- All data can be cleared in Settings
- Uninstalling the app removes all associated data

### Children's Privacy

MoodEcho does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages and contains no inappropriate content.

### Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.

### Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the App Store.

## 中文

### 简介

MoodEcho（"我们"或"本应用"）致力于保护您的隐私。本隐私政策说明了我们在您使用语音情绪日记应用时如何处理您的信息。

### 数据收集与存储

**仅本地存储**：您的所有数据仅存储在您的设备上。我们不会在外部服务器上收集、传输或存储您的任何个人数据。

本应用在本地存储以下数据：
- 语音录音（音频文件）
- 录音转写的文本
- 情绪分析结果
- 应用设置和偏好

### 语音与语音识别

- 语音录音使用 Apple 的设备端语音识别框架处理
- 在标准分析过程中，音频数据不会离开您的设备
- 录音存储在本地，可随时删除

### 可选的 AI 分析

如果您选择启用 AI 增强情绪分析：
- 您需要提供自己的 API 密钥（OpenAI、Claude、DeepSeek 或通义千问）
- 文本内容（非音频）会发送到您选择的 AI 服务进行分析
- 我们不会在您的设备之外存储或访问您的 API 密钥
- 请参阅相应 AI 提供商的隐私政策了解其数据处理方式

### 数据共享

我们不会：
- 出售您的个人数据
- 与第三方共享您的数据
- 将您的数据用于广告
- 收集分析或使用数据

### 数据删除

您可以随时删除所有数据：
- 可在应用内删除单条记录
- 可在设置中清除所有数据
- 卸载应用会删除所有相关数据

### 儿童隐私

MoodEcho 不会故意收集 13 岁以下儿童的信息。本应用适合所有年龄段，不包含任何不当内容。

### 政策变更

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。任何更改都将在上方的"最后更新"日期中体现。

### 联系方式

如果您对本隐私政策有疑问，请通过 App Store 与我们联系。
